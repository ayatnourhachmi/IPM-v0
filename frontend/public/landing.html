IPM Engine: AI-Powered Innovation Progress Model

IPM is an AI-powered innovation intake platform. Business stakeholders submit a pitch, the system auto-classifies it with NLP, detects duplicates via vector similarity, and routes the initiative through Sourcing, Qualification, and Delivery.

The Problem

Innovation intake is fragmented and slow

Business stakeholders submit free-text pitches, which are often lost or duplicated. IPM auto-classifies them with NLP (objective, impact, category, context), detects duplicates, and routes the initiative through a user-driven pipeline across three phases: Sourcing -> Qualification -> Delivery.

The Solution

IPM: The 3-Phase Innovation Pipeline

A structured workflow with 3 Stage Gates ensures only the best ideas move forward. Sourcing (Discovery) -> Qualification (Evaluation) -> Delivery (Selection). Every stage is supported by AI to accelerate decision making.

AI-Powered Sourcing & Classification

Pitch your idea with free-text and select a temporal horizon. AI automatically extracts the business objective, expected impact, category, and context/constraints.

Multi-Source Discovery

Explore internal catalogs, tech signals, startups, and watch feeds to find relevant solutions. Refine searches dynamically with AI context to narrow down the best tech partners for your initiative.

Rigorous Evaluation Setup

Compare selected solutions side-by-side. Score them on fit, feasibility, cost, and innovation to make an informed decision at the SG-2 gate.

How We Work

IPM Engine blends intuitive submission, AI-powered classification, and rigorous stage gates into a continuous innovation pipeline. The platform analyzes your pitch, maps it to the right horizon, and coordinates Discovery, Evaluation, and Selection.

Step 1

Step 1 — Business Need

Pitch + Horizon → AI fills objective, impact, category, context. Fields are editable before submit. Creates DRAFT status.

Step 2

Step 2 — Discovery

4-panel grid: DXC Catalog, Tech Signals, Startups, Tech Watch. Select items for Evaluation. Stage Gate 1: GO / REWORK / STOP.

Step 3

Step 3 — Evaluation

Side-by-side comparison of selected Discovery items. Scoring grid: fit, feasibility, cost, innovation. Stage Gate 2: GO / REWORK / STOP.

Step 4

Step 4 — Selection & Recos

Select exactly 1 solution. Generate AI-powered recommendation document. Export as PDF/DOCX. Stage Gate 3: GO to Delivery phase.
